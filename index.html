How far can I go with one battery charge? What factors have an effect on range?

The range depends on many factors. In addition to battery capacity, the selected level of engine assistance, geographical conditions, road surface, pedal frequency, and not least the weight of the rider and their luggage as well as tyre pressure all play a decisive role. Low temperatures in winter mean that your range will be reduced. Charge your battery inside and only attach it to your e-bike shortly before you set off.
